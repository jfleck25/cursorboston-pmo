# Project Brief: Momentum (The Anti-PM Shipping Engine)

## Vision
Momentum is an AI-first, gamified project management tool designed for rapid-shipping developer cohorts. It replaces the friction of traditional B2B SaaS with the dopamine loops of an arcade game, focusing on movement, accountability, and collective energy.

---

## Core Visual Identity
- **Theme:** Deep Obsidian (#0F1115) background with high-contrast neon accents.
- **Primary Color:** Neon Cyber-Green (#00FF66) for "Ship" actions and success states.
- **Secondary Color:** Electric Blue (#00F0FF) for navigation and active borders.
- **Typography:** JetBrains Mono for data/numbers; Inter for UI text.
- **Aesthetic:** "IDE meets Arcade Cabinet." Tactical, technical, and high-energy.

---

## Key Features & Views

### 1. Command Center & Radar (The Pulse)
- **Cohort Fuel Gauge:** A spaceship-thruster styled progress bar that fills as the cohort ships.
- **Live Radar Map:** A concentric circular map where users move closer to the center ("Shipped") as they complete tasks.
- **Streak Tracking:** Dynamic flame icons representing consecutive days active.

### 2. The Assembly Line (The Kanban Killer)
- **Conveyor Belt UI:** A horizontal board with an animated striped background implying forward momentum.
- **Zones:** Incoming → In the Works → Shipped.
- **Card Interaction:** Cards pulse and lock into "Neon Green" state upon shipping, triggering celebratory animations.

### 3. The Slot Machine (Task Generator)
- **Gamified Intake:** A 3D arcade-style modal used to generate new tasks.
- **Three-Reel System:** [Source] + [Tech/Scope] + [Output] spins to create a unique shipping goal.
- **Physicality:** Large tactile "PUSH" button with neon lighting and mechanical sound-feel.

### 4. Friday Launch (The Showcase)
- **Voting Board:** A celebratory grid of "Launch Cards" highlighting weekly builds.
- **Social Mechanics:** Upvote arrows and "Fire" emoji reactions for peer recognition.
- **Reveal Phase:** Admin-triggered "Reveal Winner" for high-stakes competition.

---

## Technical Constraints
- **Framework:** React/Next.js with Tailwind CSS.
- **Animations:** Framer Motion for reel spins, conveyor movement, and state transitions.
- **Language:** English (Default).
- **Device:** Desktop-first (IDE-optimized).

---

## Success Metrics
- **Velocity:** Increase in weekly ships per user.
- **Engagement:** Daily interactions with the Slot Machine and Radar.
- **Sentiment:** Qualitative feedback on "shipping friction" vs. "momentum dopamine."
